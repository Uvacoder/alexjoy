Actor. Fitness. Wellness. Human.

ACTING

Alex Joy is an actor, writer and comedienne living in NYC. Favorite past roles include Helena in the Young Company’s A Midsummer NIght’s Dream at Classic Stage Company and Jill Mason in Equus. She’s an Acting MFA graduate from Columbia University (go CUGAs).

WELLNESS

Alex Joy has been in the wellness world for 10 years. She is a certified Holistic Health Coach through IIN, a meditation teacher through the Interdependence Project, and has taught indoor cycling for 6 years in two countries. She teaches a pop-up meditation and movement experiences called “Self-Care Sunday” at HUB seventeen, lululemon’s community space, quarterly in NYC. She also works with The Nicholas Pratley Wellness Experience as an Elev8 Business Consultant for wellness and fitness start-ups.
